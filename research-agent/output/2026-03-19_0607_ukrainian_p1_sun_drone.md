# Ukrainian P1-SUN Drone

## Introduction / Overview
The P1-SUN is a Ukrainian interceptor drone developed by the defense technology company SkyFall. It is designed to protect Ukrainian airspace by intercepting and destroying enemy drones and other aerial threats. The drone features a modular design, a 3D-printed airframe, and capabilities suitable for modern multi-layered air defense systems.

## Development and Design
- Developed by Ukrainian company SkyFall.
- Modular design with a 3D-printed airframe.
- Initially capable of speeds up to 300 km/h, later upgraded by 50% to approximately 450 km/h.
- Operational ceiling up to 5,000 meters.
- Most components (~85%) sourced domestically within Ukraine.
